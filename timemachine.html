Ready for the magic? The Time Machine

Welcome aboard our Time Machine! In this section, the user can view six different articles proposed by us in as many typographic styles inspired by specific historical periods. The following different representations of texts were created taking inspiration from magazines, documents and other sources of that time, in order to be as accurate and realistic as possible.

Ready to go Back To The Future? Hereâ€™s how it works:

Immediately below these instructions, the user will find a Timeline which covers the period of time elapsed between the creation of documents with the Sumerian cuneiform script in 3200BC and what our team imagined to be the website of the future, in 2025. By clicking on one of these six time points, the tool will prepare to show the text in the style corresponding to that historical period. To select articles - which are located under the timeline - the user simply needs to click on one of them, from the ART. 1 to ART. 6, and the website will automatically load it in the required style.

Very simple, isn't it? Then let's start!
