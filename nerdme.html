A spot for all the nerdy-typo-travellers WE KNOW YOU HAVE A NERDY SIDE

Being nerdy is something between vocation and individual nature. You can be born or you can become one. What we are certain of is that the life of the nerd is full of interesting things. If you landed here, we are sure we will awaken the nerdy side we know is in you.

The real nerd is a wonderful creature. There are many chances that he/she accompanied Frodo on his journey to Middle-earth, did magic with Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, spent his childhood hurting his mother's feet by disseminating LEGO bricks in his room and, of course, spent nights and days studying Klingon.

If you know what we are talking about (well, at least we know you didn't study Klingon, did you?), this is the right spot for you. We have taken a shorter timeline, composed by four dots, each of them hosting a different nerdy style. We played a bit with Javascript and, therefore, we also got help from few codes we found online. We present here:

  • Star Wars: a reworking of Christopher Kade's code;
  • Super Mario: a reworking of Matt Seymour's code;
  • Harry Potter: a reworking of Mahmoud Zakaria's code;
  • Lego: entirely thanks to Dan Harding's Pen.

So, have a good time and may the Force be with you!
