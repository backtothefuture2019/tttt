A journey under the sea About the VISIT

This project was developed for the course 'Information Technologies and Knowledge Management" of the University of Bologna, a.y. 2018/2019. The main purpose is to create an interactive website that can be used by the user as a tool able to display texts and images with many different typographic styles, each inspired by a different historical period. The visitor is enabled, by simply clicking on any point of the timeline, to "travel through time" from the age of the Sumerians in 3200 BC, up to the website of the future, in 2040. Our attention has focused on some crucial points in the history of typography, which include the invention of the typewriter in 1878, the foundation of the Bauhaus school of art, architecture and design in 1919, the blooming of the artistic-cultural movement of the Pop Art in 1960, and the golden age of arcade video games in 1980. Enjoy your trip in our Time Machine!
